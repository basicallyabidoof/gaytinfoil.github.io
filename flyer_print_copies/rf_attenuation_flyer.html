ABOUT RF ATTENUATION

WHAT IS RF ATTENUATION?

RF attenuation measures how well materials block radio signals, measured in decibels (dB). Higher dB = better blocking.

FARADAY SHIELDING BASICS

Conductive materials like aluminum foil block electromagnetic fields. No gaps = better shielding. Any opening larger than 1/10th wavelength allows signal leakage.

DESIGN TIPS

• Use multiple layers of foil
• Ensure good contact between layers
• Minimize gaps and seams
• Seal openings with conductive tape

FREQUENCY GUIDE

WiFi (2.4 GHz): Small gaps matter
FM Radio (88.5 MHz): Large gaps OK
Higher frequencies = harder to shield
